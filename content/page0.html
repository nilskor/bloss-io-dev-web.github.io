Introducing bloss.
An operating system for you.

We at Nilskör want to make technology an engaging, rich experience that enables your daily activities. The result of our initial work investment is the release of Version 1 of the bloss Operating System (OS) , codenamed the 'Fennec' release. Its foundations have a great depth of stability, robustness and application availability.

Of course, you've had an abundance of choice for years regarding whatever OS to run on your machines, but at times it seems that stability or being allowed to decide simple things like update schedules are out of your control.

Do something new.
Install bloss OS.

GRAPHICS

High DPI/4K Support

bloss supports HiDPI displays. The extra crispness adds to the already well defined user experience. Read about how to enable Hi-DPI support in the apps that you build. More developer documentation here.

INTERNATIONAL LANGUAGES

Unicode Localization

bloss uses UTF-8 encoding for all of the text. This ensures that you can develop the app in your native language or any other language around the world.

PERFORMANCE

Hardware Accelerated Rendering

bloss OS will always use the most efficient way to draw your applications, to maximize performance and take advantage of your hardware.

INTERACTION

Gestures and Movement

In bloss, pointer and touch events are handled seamlessly, making your apps work on various kinds of input devices out of the box.

PRE-INSTALLED APPS

Your Work, Your Play, Your Way!

At the heart of Nilskör's vision for bloss OS is that it should immediately be usable by anyone. We have included different apps that serve the same purpose simply because we believe in giving you many choices. For example, we provide Firefox and Chrome as two very popular and capable web browsers to get you were you want to go (and interestingly, there's a third option buried in the OS as well!).
