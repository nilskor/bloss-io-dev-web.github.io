Introducing bloss.
An operating system for you.

We at Nilskör want to make technology an engaging, rich experience that enables your daily activities. The result of our initial work investment is the release of Version 1 of the  bloss Operating System (OS), codenamed the 'Fennec' release. Its foundations have a great depth of stability, robustness and application availability.

Of course, you've had an abundance of choice for years regarding whatever OS to run on your machines, but at times it seems that stability or being allowed to decide simple things like update schedules are out of your control.

Do something new.
Install bloss OS.

GRAPHICS

High DPI/4K Support

bloss supports Hi-DPI displays. The extra crispness adds to the already well defined user experience. Read about how to enable  Hi-DPI  support in the apps that you build. More developer documentation here.

INTERNATIONAL LANGUAGES

Unicode Localization

bloss uses UTF-8 encoding for all of its text. This ensures that you can develop apps in your native language, or any other language around the world.

PERFORMANCE

Hardware Accelerated Rendering

bloss OS will always use the most efficient way to render your applications, to maximize performance and take advantage of your available hardware.

INTERACTION

Gestures and Movement

In bloss, pointer and touch events are handled seamlessly, making your apps work on various kinds of input devices out of the box.

PRE-INSTALLED APPS

Your Work, Your Play, Your Way!

Our vision for bloss OS is that it should be immediately usable by anyone. Included are different apps that serve the same purpose, just simply because! For example, we provide Firefox and Chrome as two very popular and capable web browsers to get you were you want to go.

CONTEMPORARY

Unapologetically Modern

The future of computing currently sits with 64 bit CPU's and software. Don't expect bloss to run on an ancient PC. Your target device should be hi-res, 4GB or more of RAM, at least 40 GB of storage and so on. Something built from 2015 onwards, as that's already half a decade ago!

BUSINESS

Servers & VMs

It's no surprise that the global dominance of smartphones sit on top operating systems that have Unix DNA. And why is that? Because 24x7 uptime is entrusted to be run on these OSes.
