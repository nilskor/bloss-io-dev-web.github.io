About Nilskör

 
Nilskör is a young, dynamic company born in Australia.
 
Its vision for the future is simple - make tech easier to use.
 
We are a people first company, and that's the whole point. We want people to be out enjoying their lives, doing what they love the best. The moment that technology gets in the way, or takes hours to sort out the ridiculously easy things, we're on the wrong path.
 
The first steps to realising our vision are:
  1. an operating system as the platform to deliver our services and products
  2. documentation, specifically, clear and easy to understand documentation
 
The operating system is named bloss , and it is a Linux based product. And that's all we're going to say. There's no need for infinite discussion about which flavour of OS is better or worse than another. All we want recognised is that Linux has a place on the desktop as well as Windows and macOS, and that it can be a beautiful experience. Hence, beautiful Linux operating system becomes b.l.OS or simply bloss!
 
Additionally, we are keen to provide bloss already pre-installed on tablets, laptops and PCs, as well as provide support services for customer installations.
 
The future roadmap includes AI, and we're excited about our internal models and what they might be capable of in the near future.
